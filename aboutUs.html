About Merci

Merci is a luxury car dealership in Indonesia, with headquarters in Cirebon. As a car dealership, the business first opened its doors in 2011. Merci has worked hard to establish itself as one of Indonesia's leading car dealerships.

Merci has continually provided the highest quality cars and services to its customers over the past five years.

It is our pleasure to serve you.

Founder: Phd. Nick Young (1971)

Nick Young was born in Jakarta, Indonesia, in 1971. He opened Merci in 2011 and quickly established a reputation for automotive quality and service. His four sons also become employees of Merci.
